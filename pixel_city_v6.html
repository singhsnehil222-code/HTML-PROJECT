💡 STRATEGY TIPS
🌴Start by clearing the jungle! Use 💥 Demolish on trees — each earns $15–$35. Clear space before building.
🏭Build Factories & Shops first — coins arc directly to your wallet every 4 seconds, Hay Day style!
🪙Hit the 💹 ECONOMY tab → Collect Mayor Bonus ($50) every 30 seconds for free money!
🌳Parks come with 4 tree types, a bench, and a night lamp — they glow beautifully at night!
🛣️Lay Roads to get street lamps (auto on at night) and unlock cars + walking citizens.
🏙️Skyscraper unlocks when you have 💰1500 saved — not by population!
